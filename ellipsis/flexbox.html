Notes

  • The default value of `flex` property is: initial (0 1 auto)
  • 0 (flex-grow): The item will not grow to take up more space in the container.
  • 1 (flex-shrink): The item will shrink if necessary to avoid overflowing the container.
  • auto (flex-basis): The initial size of the item is based on its content or width/height properties.
  • In general, the flex-grow and flex-shrink properties distribute leftover or excess space proportionally among flex items. However, when you set flex: n (which is shorthand for flex: n 1 0), the flex-basis is 0 instead of auto. This means that initially, the item won't take up any space until it grows based on the available space.
